The First AI Workshop is a Wrap! See What We Covered

Published: August 22, 2025 | By: Mgr. Gabriel Kožík

On Friday, August 22, the first workshop focused on the practical use of Google's AI tools took place at my office in Brno. A great group of curious people came together, and the atmosphere was absolutely fantastic. The goal was simple – to bust some myths and show how artificial intelligence can genuinely save us hours of work and open up new creative possibilities.

From Theory to Practice in One Morning

We started with the most important thing: explaining that AI is not an all-knowing Google but rather a creative assistant whose outputs need to be verified. We discussed the risks of "hallucinations" and showed why a well-formulated query (prompt) is the foundation of everything.

Then we dove into practice. Participants got to try how to:

  • Use Gemini to quickly analyze the market or prepare research from dozens of sources.
  • Create their own knowledge base in NotebookLM that draws only from uploaded documents (contracts, podcasts, books) and provides answers without the risk of making things up.
  • Integrate AI with Google Workspace to summarize emails or schedule meetings directly in the calendar.
  • Compose an original song in a minute using Suno AI, which we thoroughly enjoyed.
What made me happiest was seeing the participants have that "aha" moment when they realized how they could immediately use a specific tool in their business or personal life. Those moments are why I do this.

What's Next?

The huge interest has convinced me to continue with the workshop series. The first one was a great start, and I'm already planning the next one.

Did you miss it? No problem! The next date is already set for September 19, 2025. If you want to take your efficiency to the next level and learn to master tools that will give you an edge, don't miss it.
